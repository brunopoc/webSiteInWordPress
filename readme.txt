Find out how you can customize the theme in a few steps:

Go to: Appearance —> Theme Options :

General option:
--------------
Logo:
-----

Change Logo sections :

Logo image – Choose an image that fits to your site.
Logo text – Put the description that fits to your logo.

Download brochure:
------------------


Change Download brochure sections :

Select page – Select the page or the pages where you want this sections to appear. *Hold down the control (ctrl) button to select multiple pages.

Archive page – Show or hide download brochure section in the archive page.

Search page – Show or hide download brochure section in the search page.

Download brochure text – Enter a text to appear in the left side of the download brochure button.

Title for the Download brochure button – Enter a title to appear on the download brochure button.

Link for the Download brochure button – Enter the link of the download brochure button.


Slider:
-------

Change Slider sections :

Select page – Select the page or the pages where you want this sections to appear. *Hold down the control (ctrl) button to select multiple pages.

Archive page – Show or hide slider section in the archive page.

Search page – Show or hide slider section in the search page.

Slider big title – Add a big title for your slider

Slider title – Add a title for your slider

Slider subtitle – Add a subtile for your slider

Slide image(s) – Add the image or the images that you want to appear in your slider.


Our services:
------------

Change Our services sections :

Select page – Select the page or the pages where you want this sections to appear. *Hold down the control (ctrl) button to select multiple pages.

Archive page – Show or hide our services section in the archive page.

Search page – Show or hide our services section in the search page.

Image – Select the service image.

Title – Enter the service title.

Text – Enter the service description (text)


Our team:
---------

Change Our team sections :

Select page – Select the page or the pages where you want this sections to appear. *Hold down the control (ctrl) button to select multiple pages.

Archive page – Show or hide our team section in the archive page.

Search page – Show or hide our team section in the search page.

Person image – Choose the person picture to appear in the our team area.

Person name – Enter the person name to appear in the our team area

Person job – Enter the person job to appear in the our team area


Testimonials:
-------------

Change Testimonials sections :

Select page – Select the page or the pages where you want this sections to appear. *Hold down the control (ctrl) button to select multiple pages.

Archive page – Show or hide testimonials section in the archive page.

Search page – Show or hide testimonials section in the search page.

Title – Enter the title what you want to appear in the testimonials area.

Content – Enter the text what you want to appear in the testimonials area.

Author – Add the author name to appear in the testimonials area

Info about the author – Enter a small piece of information to appear after the author name in the testimonials area

Our skill:
-----------

Change Our skill sections :

Select page – Select the page or the pages where you want this sections to appear. *Hold down the control (ctrl) button to select multiple pages.

Archive page – Show or hide download brochure section in the archive page.

Search page – Show or hide download brochure section in the search page.

Title – Add the title to appear in the skills area.

Skill text – Add the text what you want to appear in the box of skill area.

Skill value – Add the value what you want for the box of skill area.


Blog+Search+Archive option
---------------------------

Layout for blog page  – Choose the layout for the blog page.

Featured image – Show or hide featured image in the blog page.

Date – Show or hide date in the blog page, the archive page and the search page.

Category – Show or hide category in the blog page, the archive page and the search page.

Author – Show or hide author in the blog page, the archive page and the search page.

Tags – Show or hide tags in the blog page, the archive page and the search page.


In Single post option, you can change:
-------------------------------------

Layot for single post – Choose the layout for the single post.

Featured Image – Show or hide featured image in the single post.

Date – Show or hide date in the single post.

Category – Show or hide category in the single post.

Author – Show or hide author in the single post.

Tags – Show or hide tags in the single post

Comments – Show or hide comments in the single post.


Footer option, here you can change:
-----------------------------------

Footer columns – Select how many columns should be displayed in your footer.

Logo – You can choose any picture you think it fits to your website.

Logo footer text – The text in the footer for logo.

Twitter link – Enter your twitter account link.

RSS link – Enter your RSS account.

Linkedin link – Enter your Linkedin account.

Copyright – Enter your copyright.



Contact options:
---------------


Here you can add your contact details and social links :

Email address – Enter your email address to appear in footer and contact page.

Phone number – Enter your phone number to appear in footer and contact page.
